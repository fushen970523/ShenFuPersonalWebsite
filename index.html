Welcome!

My name is Jiezheng Wei (in Chinese: 魏杰铮). I was born in Chibi, Hubei Province and raised up there until I was 15. And then I finished my secondary education in Wugang No.3 High School in Wuhan. Later I did my undergraduate in CEMA, Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE)

and obtained a Master degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). Now I'm doing PhD at School of Economics (SOE), Singapore Management University (SMU).

My research insterests are financial econometrics and machine learning.
